## 💰 **CALCULATION EXAMPLES**

### **Example 1: ETB 1,500 Salary**
- **Tax Bracket**: 1 (Non-Taxable)
- **Income Tax**: **ETB 0**
- **POESSA**: 1,500 × 0.07 = **ETB 105**
- **Net Pay**: 1,500 - 0 - 105 = **ETB 1,395**

### **Example 2: ETB 3,000 Salary**
- **Tax Bracket**: 2 (15%)
- **Income Tax**: (3,000 × 0.15) - 300 = 450 - 300 = **ETB 150**
- **POESSA**: 3,000 × 0.07 = **ETB 210**
- **Net Pay**: 3,000 - 150 - 210 = **ETB 2,640**

### **Example 3: ETB 5,000 Salary**
- **Tax Bracket**: 3 (20%)
- **Income Tax**: (5,000 × 0.20) - 500 = 1,000 - 500 = **ETB 500**
- **POESSA**: 5,000 × 0.07 = **ETB 350**
- **Net Pay**: 5,000 - 500 - 350 = **ETB 4,150**

### **Example 4: ETB 8,000 Salary**
- **Tax Bracket**: 4 (25%)
- **Income Tax**: (8,000 × 0.25) - 850 = 2,000 - 850 = **ETB 1,150**
- **POESSA**: 8,000 × 0.07 = **ETB 560**
- **Net Pay**: 8,000 - 1,150 - 560 = **ETB 6,290**

### **Example 5: ETB 12,000 Salary**
- **Tax Bracket**: 5 (30%)
- **Income Tax**: (12,000 × 0.30) - 1,350 = 3,600 - 1,350 = **ETB 2,250**
- **POESSA**: 12,000 × 0.07 = **ETB 840**
- **Net Pay**: 12,000 - 2,250 - 840 = **ETB 8,910**

### **Example 6: ETB 18,000 Salary** *(Your Previous Example)*
- **Tax Bracket**: 6 (35%)

- **POESSA**: Min(18,000, 15,000) × 0.07 = **ETB 1,050** (capped)
- **Net Pay**: 18,000 - 4,250 - 1,050 = **ETB 12,700**

### **Example 7: ETB 20,000 Salary**
- **Tax Bracket**: 6 (35%)

- **POESSA**: Min(20,000, 15,000) × 0.07 = **ETB 1,050** (capped)
